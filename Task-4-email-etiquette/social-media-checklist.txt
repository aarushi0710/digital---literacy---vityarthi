Social Media Do's and Don'ts:



Do's--

* Make your profiles private, if that’s an option.
* Share articles you find interesting. It might be a good idea to have some sort of social media presence in the field you are interested in.
* Use social media to promote yourself and your business and make international connections that might be useful for you.
* Fact-check news or claims before sharing to prevent the spread of misinformation.
* Be mindful that "deleted" posts can often be recovered or screenshotted.







Don'ts--

* Avoid posting content that is offensive, discriminatory, or illegal. 
* Cyberbullying is a serious offense that can have devastating consequences for victims. Avoid harassing, threatening, or intimidating others online.
* Be cautious about sharing sensitive personal information online, such as your address, phone number, or financial details.
* Avoid posting anything online when you are under the influence of alcohol or drugs. 
* Don't use strange or inappropriate hashtags. Hashtags are often forgotten but you can also be found using them.
