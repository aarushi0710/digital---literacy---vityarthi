For this assignment, I chose Canva to make a single page infographic . I chose a basic template so that the information put in the infographic comes out more. This design specifically focuses on three areas: Useful Digital Tools (including Cool Text for design, LinkedIn for networking, and TechGig for coding practice), Email Etiquette (the "do's and don'ts" of professional reaching out), and Safe Internet Practices (like using MFA and spotting phishing).

What I found most interesting while making this was actually the "design-to-content" balance. It’s one thing to know that you need a strong password or a clear subject line, but trying to explain why that matters in under 10 words for a visual slide is a real challenge. Digital Literacy is not just about knowing these websites or apps,its about knowing how to use it and make the most out of it.
