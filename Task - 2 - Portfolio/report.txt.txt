For this digital portfolio task, I set up professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to
document my progress as an engineering student. I chose GitHub to act as a home for my technical 
projects, as it provides a clear way to show how my coding skills are improving. LinkedIn was my 
choice for professional networking; it helps me stay connected with the VIT Bhopal community and 
keep an eye on internship opportunities globally. Finally, I picked Kaggle because it is the best
place for a CSE student specializing in AI and ML to find real-world datasets and practice 
competitive data science.
Over the next four years, I plan to use these platforms to build a strong digital footprint. I will
use GitHub to store everything from my basic Python scripts to the complex AI models I hope to build
later. On LinkedIn, I want to connect with alumni and industry experts who can offer guidance as I 
prepare for my career. I also plan to use Kaggle to apply what I learn about NumPy and Pandas in 
class to actual data challenges. Together, these tools will help me transition from being a student
to a career-ready software engineer.
